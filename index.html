Welcome to Hangman!

Instructions

  1. Click 'Start Game' to begin.
  2. Guess a letter you think is in the hidden word.
  3. If your guess is correct, the letter will appear in the word.
  4. If your guess is incorrect, part of the hangman figure will be drawn.
  5. You have up to 7 incorrect guesses before you lose.
  6. Guess the entire word to win.
